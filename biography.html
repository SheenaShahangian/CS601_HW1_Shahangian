My Unexpected Path

My path in life has been anything but predictable. I graduated with an undergraduate degree in business and marketing with the assumption I wanted to work at a huge advertising agency. After living the ad agency life for a bit, I quickly realized it was not my type of place or lifestyle.

I've always been a creative soul, so in my free time in the evenings, I began looking into what it would look like to work as a photographer. After several courses and lots of online learning and research, I launched my photo business. Things took off from there, and I quickly realized that I had found my match.
